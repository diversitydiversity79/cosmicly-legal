Privacy Policy

Cosmicly: Horoscope & AI Tarot

Last updated: 13 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Cosmicly: Horoscope & AI Tarot ("Cosmicly", "the app", "we", "us") handles your information. We have built Cosmicly to be privacy-first and offline-first: almost everything happens on your device, and we do not run servers that store your personal data. Please read this policy together with our Terms of Use.

The short version:
  • Your birth details and readings are stored only on your device.
  • We do not collect your name, contacts, precise GPS location, advertising ID, or device identifiers.
  • The only time information leaves your device is when you use the Luna AI chat (it sends your message to Google's AI to generate a reply), plus a few standard technical requests described below.
  • You can erase everything at any time with Reset App Data.

1. Information stored on your device

When you set up Cosmicly and use its features, the following is created and stored locally on your device (in the app's private storage). It is used only to generate your personalized astrology, tarot, and numerology content:

  • The name you enter
  • Your date of birth, time of birth (or a default time if you choose "I don't know my exact time"), and birth city (including its approximate coordinates, looked up from an offline list bundled in the app)
  • Derived information such as your zodiac sign and numerology numbers
  • Your in-app activity and history — for example tarot draws, saved readings, saved horoscopes, your streak, and your Luna chat history

This information stays on your device. We do not have a server that receives or stores it. Your birth date and time are treated as sensitive personal information and are never sold or shared for advertising.

2. Information that leaves your device

a) Luna AI chat (Google Gemini)

Cosmicly's Luna assistant answers most questions using content built into the app. When a question cannot be answered from that built-in content, your message is sent to Google's Gemini AI service to generate a reply. In that case, the request may include:

  • The message text you typed
  • Your first name and a few astrological attributes (such as your sign, element, and numerology number) so the reply is personalized

This data is processed by Google to generate the response. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for how Google handles data sent to its AI services. If you do not wish to share chat content with Google's AI, simply do not use the Luna chat feature.

b) Optional cloud backup (Android Auto Backup)

Android may automatically back up certain app settings to your own Google Drive. Cosmicly is configured to exclude your sensitive data — your birth details and your Luna chat history are not included in automatic cloud backup. You can also disable Android backups entirely in your device settings.

c) Standard technical requests

Like most apps, Cosmicly may make routine technical requests that do not include your personal astrology data — for example loading fonts (Google Fonts) and, if you choose to rate the app, using Google Play's in-app review service. When you tap a share or external link, your device's own apps handle that action.

3. What we do NOT collect

Cosmicly does not collect or transmit:

  • Precise GPS / real-time location (city search is fully offline)
  • Your contacts, photos, or files
  • Device identifiers or advertising ID
  • Any payment information (the app is free)

5. Your control & data deletion

Because your data lives on your device, you are in control of it:

  • Reset App Data (in the app) permanently erases your profile, history, saved items, Luna chat, and all locally stored data, returning the app to a fresh state.
  • Uninstalling the app removes its on-device data.
  • You can turn off notifications and Android cloud backup at any time in your device settings.

6. Children's privacy

Cosmicly is intended for users aged 13 and older (or the minimum age of digital consent in your country). It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, contact us and we will help address it — though note that, by design, such information is stored only on that device.

7. Features that may be added in the future

To be transparent, we may introduce the following in future versions of Cosmicly. If and when we do, this policy will be updated before those features collect or transmit data, and where required we will ask for your consent:

  • Analytics (e.g. Google Analytics for Firebase) to understand, in aggregate, how the app is used and to improve it.
  • Crash reporting (e.g. Firebase Crashlytics) to detect and fix stability problems.
  • Advertising (e.g. Google AdMob). If introduced, ads may use an advertising identifier; you would be shown any required consent choices, and we would update this policy and our Play Data Safety disclosure accordingly.

As of the "last updated" date above, these features are not active and no such data is collected or transmitted.

8. Data security

Your data is stored in the app's private, sandboxed storage on your device. Information sent to Google's services (such as the Luna AI requests) is transmitted over encrypted connections. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we design Cosmicly to minimize the data that ever leaves your device.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.
